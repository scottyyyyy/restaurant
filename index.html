Our story

We are a well established, family run, Irish - Italian restaurant located in the heart of West Belfast.

Established in 1990, and with strong Irish - Italian family connections, we aim to provide all our customers with an authentic dining experience.

Voted best restaurant in West Belfast, and winners of the 'Best Of The West' award for the past 4 years running 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019.
